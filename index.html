Help & Tutorials

How to use the voice advisor?

Using the voice advisor is simple:

  1. First, fill in your farmer profile with location, crop, and sowing date.
  2. Select your preferred language from the dropdown.
  3. Tap the "Tap to Speak" button and ask your question clearly.
  4. Listen to the response and provide feedback.
  5. If needed, escalate to a human expert.
What kind of questions can I ask?

You can ask a wide variety of agricultural questions, such as:

  • Crop-specific advice (wheat, rice, cotton, etc.)
  • Watering and irrigation guidance
  • Fertilizer and nutrient management
  • Pest and disease control
  • Weather-related farming advice
  • Soil health and management
  • Harvesting and post-harvest techniques
How accurate is the advice?

Our AI advisor is trained on agricultural data from reliable sources and provides context-aware advice based on your location, crop, and season. However, farming conditions can vary, so we recommend:

  • Using the advice as a general guideline
  • Considering local conditions and practices
  • Escalating to human experts for critical issues
  • Providing feedback to help improve our system
